Produced by Satwika Reddy Guntipally

The Learned Woman

This scene is between CHRYSALE, ARISTE

SCENE II; CHRYSALE, ARISTE.

ARI.

Good day to you, brother

(softly and listening_).

CHRY.

And to you also, brother.

ARI.

Do you know what brings me here?

(_to_ ARISTE)

CHRY.

No, I do not; but I am ready to hear it, if it pleases tell me.

ARI.

You have known Clitandre for some time now?

CHRY.

Certainly; and he often comes to our house.
